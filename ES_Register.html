Welcome to the English Society!

Thank you for expressing your interest on joining this organization. This registration form acts as your official recruitment in the English Society.

There are 2 steps in the registration process.

  1. The first step is through the website, where you will choose the committees you want to join. After this step, you will get a unique code.
  2. The second step is through Microsoft Forms, where you will enter your personal information and paste the code you obtained from the first step.

For issues and concerns regarding the registration, don't hesitate to contact us through our Facebook page.

Click "START" below to begin registering!

Pick a committee!

There are 3 committees that have different activities and responsibilities in the organization. Pick one or more that suits your interests.

You can select more than one committee.

Technical Committee

The Technical Committee is responsible for the organization's technical aspects, such as managing its social media presence, documentation, and content production.

Journalism Committee

The Journalism Committee leans more into writing. They are responsible for writing journals, news articles, and event write-ups. It is also responsible for the scripts performed by our hosts during events.

Promotional Committee

The Promotional Committee is responsible for the performances of the organization in different mediums, such as singing, dancing, acting, and hosting. They are comprised of great English speakers that is able to convey the intended emotion and message in a meaningful and efficient way.
