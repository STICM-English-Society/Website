Kasya sa Frame

Written by:

PE 4 Committee

Category:

Recreational Games

Date published:

21 April 2024

Kasya sa Frame is an exciting team-based game where players work together to fit their heads within a frame at a certain height. Each round presents the team with a specific number of player heads that they must fit into the frame while ensuring they don't exceed the height limit.

Team/Player Composition

2 or more for each team (Even though the number of players may vary, number of players for each team should be equal)

Type of Tournament

Single Elimination

Game Mechanics

  • Setup
    • Frame setup
      • Set up the frame in a spacious area where players can gather around.
      • Adjust the frame to a starting height that provides a reasonable challenge for the players.
      • Ensure the frame is stable and securely placed to avoid accidents during gameplay.
    • Game area arrangement
      • Arrange chairs or standing spaces around the frame to accommodate the players.
      • Ensure there's enough room for players to move around and strategize effectively during the game.
  • Set up the music player with a playlist of upbeat songs to play during the game.
  • Designate a starting player to control the timer or stopwatch.

Gameplay

  • Players form teams and take turns fitting their heads into the frame.
  • Each round, the team is given a frame with a certain height limit and a specified number of player heads they need to fit.
  • Players must strategize and communicate effectively to position their heads within the frame without exceeding the height limit.
  • If any part of a player's head extends beyond the frame's height limit, the round is considered a failure.
  • Teams have a limited number of attempts to successfully fit all the required heads into the frame.

Time limit

  • Total duration of the game - 10 minutes
  • Total duration for each round - 2 minutes

Rounds

  • Total number of rounds - 5 rounds

Safety reminder

  • Remember, safety should be a priority during gameplay. Ensure that the frame is sturdy and stable, and players should be cautious to avoid injury while jumping.

How to win

In "Kasya sa Frame," winning the game involves successfully completing each round by fitting the specified number of player heads into the frame without touching or exceeding the sides of the frame.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • The do’s are all listed in the “How to play” section.

Don'ts

  • Don’t touch the frame
    • Players must refrain from making contact with the frame while jumping through it. Touching the frame results in elimination from the game.
  • Don’t collide with other players
    • It's essential to maintain distance and coordination with fellow players to prevent accidental collisions, which could lead to injuries or disqualification.
  • Don’t disrupt the frame holder
    • The players should be careful with handling the frame while playing the game.
  • Don’t compromise safety
    • Players should prioritize safety at all times by ensuring the frame is stable and secure and by exercising caution during jumps to avoid injuries. Any reckless behavior that puts oneself or others at risk should be avoided.
