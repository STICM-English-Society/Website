Pass the Ball

Written by:

PE 4 Committee

Category:

Recreational Games

Date published:

21 April 2024

Pass the Ball is a high-energy game where two teams of 12 players aim to pass a ball sequentially while defusing scattered time bombs. Players must pass the ball cleanly and defuse the bombs within a set time limit, showcasing teamwork and coordination. Points are awarded based on successful defusal and completion time, creating a thrilling race against the clock with a focus on fair play and sportsmanship.

Team/Player Composition

6 boys, 6 girls

Type of Tournament

Round Robin

Game Mechanics

Starting position

  • Each team lines up in a single-file formation, standing one behind the other, at the starting line.

Passing sequence

  • The game begins with a designated player from one team holding the ball. Players must pass the ball sequentially, from one player to the next, until it reaches the last player in line.

Time bomb

  • Before the game starts, the time bombs are placed at the end of the field in front of each team. Players must defuse the time bomb by spinning around the time bomb for 4 times within 15 seconds while holding the ball. Afterwards, the player will pass the ball to the next player and will do the same procedure.

Movement

  • Players may move along the field while passing the ball and defusing the time bomb but must stay within the boundaries of the playing area.

Ball handling

  • Players may move along the field while passing the ball and defusing the time bomb but must stay within the boundaries of the playing area.

Completion of passing sequence

  • Once the ball reaches the last player in line, that player carries or passes the ball to the designated end point while also defusing any remaining time bombs along the way.

Time limit

  • 15 seconds for each player.
  • Completing the passing sequence (including the defusing of time bomb): 6 minutes for each team.

How to win

Points can be awarded based on the number of time bombs successfully defused and the shortest time to complete the tasks.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • The do’s are all listed in the “How to play” section.

Don'ts

  • Failure to defuse time bomb

    • If a player fails to complete the required spinning motion around the time bomb within the designated time limit of 15 seconds, their team incurs a penalty. This violation could result in a deduction of points and additional time added to their completion time.

  • Out of bounds movement

    • Players must remain within the boundaries of the playing area while passing the ball and defusing the time bombs. If a player steps out of bounds, whether intentionally or unintentionally, their team is penalized, and they may need to restart the passing sequence from the beginning.

  • Interference with opponent’s passing sequence

    • Deliberate interference with the opponent's passing sequence, such as blocking passes or disrupting the defusal of time bombs, constitutes a violation of fair play. Teams found guilty of interference may face penalties, including point deductions or disqualification from the game.
