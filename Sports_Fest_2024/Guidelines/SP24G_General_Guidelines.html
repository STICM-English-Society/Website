Schedule of Events

Uniforms

Every member of the team is required to wear the STI College Marikina 2024 Sport Fest Shirt. Players who do not wear the STI College Marikina 2024 Sports Fest Shirt, as well as those players who do not wear proper gear during physical sports (e.g., rubber shoes), are not allowed to play in any games that they are participating in.

Attendance

  1. Every team is required to collect the attendance of their members every day. This attendance shall be submitted to the committee head after the Sports Fest 2024 event is done.
  2. The total attendance shall match the total days that the event is held. (E.g., if the event is held for three days, then every team shall submit a 3-day copy of attendance for their team). The attendance consists of name, section, student number, time in, time out, and signature.
  3. Designated spots located around the venue will be allotted to each team where team members will be taking their attendance.

Facilities and equipment

STI College Marikina provides the facilities and necessary equipment for the event. Students are expected to use the provided equipment responsibly. If any equipment is damaged by students, they are responsible for repairing or replacing it.

Scoring and awards

  • Champion: 6 points
  • 1st Runner Up: 5 points
  • 2nd Runner Up: 4 points
  • 3rd Runner Up: 3 points
  • 4th Runner Up: 2 points
  • 5th Runner Up: 1 points

The awards ceremony will take place on the final day of the sports fest after all sports events and cheer dance performances have concluded. The officiating officials of each sport will collect the points. After totaling the points from all games, the points of each team will be added together to determine the overall winner of the Sports Fest 2024.

Code of Conduct

We're excited to have you join us for a fun celebration of sportsmanship and competition. To make sure everyone has a great time and stays safe, we've made some rules about how we should behave. If we all follow these rules, we can make sure everyone feels included and has a good experience.

  1. Respect: Treat everyone with respect, including fellow participants, coaches, referees, volunteers, and spectators. Be courteous and considerate at all times.
  2. Fair Play: Play by the rules and accept decisions made by officials. Avoid cheating, dishonesty, and unsportsmanlike conduct.
  3. Teamwork: Whether you're competing individually or as part of a team, prioritize teamwork. Support and encourage your teammates, and demonstrate good sportsmanship towards opponents.
  4. Positive attitude: Maintain a positive attitude, even in the face of setbacks or losses. Use constructive feedback as an opportunity for growth, both personally and as a team.
  5. Safety first: Prioritize safety at all times. Follow safety guidelines provided by coaches and officials. Use and wear proper gear to minimize the risk of injury. Kindly tell the committee officials if you observe unsafe behavior or conditions.
  6. Inclusion: Avoid discrimination, harassment, or bullying in any form. Create an environment where everyone feels valued and included.
  7. Good sportsmanship: Display good sportsmanship before, during, and after competitions.
  8. Responsibility: Take responsibility for your actions both on and off the court. Be punctual, arrive prepared, and fulfill your commitments to your team and committees. Clean up after yourself and respect the facilities and equipment provided.
  9. Cleanliness: Each student shares a role in keeping the venue of the event clean and orderly. Dispose trash to its proper waste bins, refrain from littering, and practice the Clean-As-You-Go rule during the event.
  10. Enjoyment: Have fun and enjoy the experience! Sport Fest is a celebration of physical activity, camaraderie, and healthy competition. Cherish the memories created and the friendships forged during this event.
  11. Role model: Lead by example and be a positive role model for others. Demonstrate exemplary behavior and uphold the values of sportsmanship, integrity, and respect. Inspire others how to be fair, honest, and respectful.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Participation is open to all students who do not have PE4. Participants must register their names to their team leader regarding the sports they would like to join before the event.
  2. Every member is required to attend the parade on the first day.
  3. All participants are expected to uphold the principles of fair play and sportsmanship. Any form of cheating, misconduct, or unsportsmanlike behavior will result in disqualification.
  4. Every player must wear proper attire.
  5. All students who will practice outside the campus are required to have their parents sign the training permission waiver.
  6. All students 21 and below must have their parent or guardian sign the provided parental consent form for cheer dance practices
  7. Every player, coach, and spectator MUST follow the code of conduct.
  8. All participants must be present at the designated time and location for their respective events. Arriving 30 minutes late may result in disqualification unless the team has a reserve player available to compete.
  9. Each sport will follow specific guidelines provided by the committee. This may include elimination rounds, timed events, or other formats. Participants must familiarize themselves with the rules of their respective events.
  10. Participants need to make sure that all the gear they use in the events meets safety rules. If officials find any gear unsafe or not right, it won't be allowed.
  11. Participants may file a protest regarding rule interpretation or officiating decisions. For team games, the team leader / captain must be the one to file the formal complaint.
  12. Scoring for each event will be determined by committee officials and judges. Results will be announced shortly after each event ends, and any disagreements must be addressed following the protest procedure. Once the team captains / leaders have signed the official score result, it is considered final and no more protests may be done by the participants.
  13. Medical assistance will be available onsite for any participants requiring medical attention during the event. All participants must disclose any pre-existing medical conditions or injuries to their team leader before participating in any games.
  14. Event organizers reserve the right to disqualify any participant found to be in violation of the rules and regulations outlined herein. Appeals may be made following the protest procedure.
  15. Players who do not wear proper attire during a game are not allowed to play.
  16. All participants share a responsibility at keeping the order and cleanliness of the venue. Students must dispose of their trash on the proper trash bins. Littering in and around the event venue is prohibited.
  17. The committee reserves the right to modify these rules and regulations as necessary to ensure the safe and fair conduct of the Sports Fest.
