I Can Hear Your Voice! The English Speech Fest 2022 Speech Choir Competition
The Speech Choir competition entitled "I Can Hear Your Voice! The English Speech Fest 2022" is the English Society's English Month event for 2022 which involves five Senior High School sections performing their speech choir pieces, along with intermission performances from various STI College Marikina students in between said pieces. <br> Along with Russel John Ang, the photographer for the event, several students also helped edit and apply watermarks on the photos featured below. These students include: Althea Sambajon, Ashly Soliman, Athena Aglipay, Casandra Doctolero, Cathy Basarte, Janine Baltazar, Larie Jane Cabunagan, and Ralph Andrae. <br> Video performances of each group and intermission performers are also available in our YouTube channel <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL_jOJQLXJOrKdDqPH460AQ4Zgls8Hjucn"> <u> here. </u> </a>
