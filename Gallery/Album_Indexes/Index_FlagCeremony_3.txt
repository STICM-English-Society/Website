Brgy. Concepcion Uno and STI College Marikina Flag Raising Ceremony
STI College Marikina Tertiary Student Representatives, along with Dean Mr. Yulo, Faculty Teachers Mr. Pascual and Mr. Cruz, and Student Affairs Officer Mr. Tocol joins the Brgy. Concepcion Uno Officials and Congresswoman Hon. Stella Quimbo on their Flah Ceremony this Monday morning. The Inauguration and Thanksgiving of the Brgy. Concepcion Uno G.S.O. building followed afterwards.
