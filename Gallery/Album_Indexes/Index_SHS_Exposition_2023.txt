SHS Exposition 2023
The SHS Exposition 2023, held at Riverbanks Marikina, is the final school event of School Year 2022 - 2023, and is the event where SHS students showcase their research outputs and products. It is also the event where various groups of students defended their research project in front of a set of panelists.
