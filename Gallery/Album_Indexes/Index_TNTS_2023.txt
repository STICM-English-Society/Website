STI College Marikina Tagisan ng Talino at Sining 2023
The yearly Tagisan ng Talino at Sining (also known as TNTS) is a variety of competitions held by STI campuses to challenge students' knowledge on their courses and skills on their crafts. With competitions such as Stand and Deliver, Code Fest, ThinkQuest, etc., students are able to show and improve their skills and showcase it to others.
