Ahh yes, February...

February is a notable month to a lot of us. For couples, they look forward to this month's Valentine's Day where they can sweetly express their love to their partner. Perhaps with tiny gestures such as hugging or holding hands, or maybe a fabulous date complete with flowers and tasty food. But to some of us single people, February might be a month of bitterness as they see couples everywhere. Whatever we think about February, we sure do have a lot of thoughts and sentiments into this month of love and leap days.

Thoughts? Message to your secret crush? Hugot lines?

February Sentiments is here to give you an outlet to share your shower thoughts, a sweet message to your crush who you secretly admire, and perhaps even your hugot lines that exudes bitterness to this month. English or Tagalog sentiments, it's all welcome! We'll post your anonymous thoughts in our social media pages for everyone to see.

How to share your Feb Sentiments anonymously

Sharing your thoughts is easy. Simply answer the Microsoft Forms linked above and fill out the necessary details. The Microsoft Form contains:

  • A text field for your February Sentiment
  • An optional alias text field

You can access the form by clicking the blue "Microsoft Form" button above.

What you can share

We encourage you to share your thoughts and feelings this February. Here's some examples of what you can share with everyone:

  • Valentine's greetings to your loved one
  • A message to your crush / a person you admire
  • Thoughts and feelings about life and academics

These can be shared in either English, Tagalog, Taglish, you name it!

What you can't share

There are some statements that you shouldn't share with us. Including any of the following along with other related cases are prohibited on sharing messages:

  • Derogatory or slanderous statements about a person, group, or organization
  • Any name / nickname that pertains to a specific person outside and inside the school
  • Private information such as addresses, numbers, etc.
  • Statements that are Not Safe For Work (NSFW)

Privacy concerns

As mentioned earlier in the article, sharers will remain anonymous as their messages are posted online.

  • Only those that are under the @marikina.sti.edu.ph email address will be able to access the form.
  • Your email will not be tracked and saved as you submit the form.
  • Messages with names and other sensitive information will not be posted.
  • The form does not require you to fill out the "Alias" field, however, we discourage you to write your actual name / nickname if you ever decide to provide one.
